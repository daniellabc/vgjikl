    The cherry blossom – sakura – is the unofficial national flower of Japan and an image of the flower appears on the Japanese 100 yen coin.


    Cherry blossom festivals are celebrated in Japan every spring and are a must-see if you’re visiting the country around March and April. From outdoor picnics to dedicated photo shoots, the cherry blossom season is enjoyed in a number of wonderful ways.
