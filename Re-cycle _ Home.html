Re-cycle

Re-cycle offers eco-friendly, guided cycling tours around Stavern during the summer

Re-cycle was made for being one part of the million parts needed to help save the world. The consept consist of using bikes donated from the local habbitats from Stavern, and the food included in the tours are collected from the resteraunts that had some leftovers. Making this company sustainable and economic!
